Color Bridge Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

```

Color Bridge is a free browser game by Hesvyn Play. This Privacy Policy explains how Color Bridge may store and use limited gameplay data.

Account Requirement

Color Bridge does not require players to create an account.

Gameplay Data We May Store

Color Bridge may store non-sensitive gameplay data to provide game features such as leaderboards, high scores, achievements, daily challenges, saved preferences, and fair ranking systems.

This data may include:

  • Nickname
  • Score
  • Anonymous player ID
  • Gameplay session ID
  • Play time
  • Blocks cleared
  • Max combo
  • Selected game mode
  • Ranking period
  • High score
  • Achievements
  • Daily challenge progress
  • Mute setting and simple preferences

Nickname Notice

Players should not enter their real name, email address, phone number, address, or other personal information as their nickname.

Leaderboards

Leaderboard data may be processed using third-party backend services such as Supabase. This data is used to display rankings, maintain fair leaderboards, prevent abuse, and detect invalid score submissions.

Local Storage and Platform Storage

Color Bridge may use local storage and/or CrazyGames Data to save progress, settings, achievements, daily challenge progress, high scores, and similar gameplay information.

Analytics and Technical Information

We may collect limited technical or usage information, such as browser type, device type, approximate region, gameplay interactions, performance information, and error information. This helps us improve game performance and user experience.

Advertising

Where applicable, advertisements may be displayed through platform-approved advertising systems or third-party advertising providers.

Children’s Privacy

Color Bridge is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and remove it if necessary.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information related to this game. However, no internet service, website, or online database can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
```
