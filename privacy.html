Last updated: June 23, 2026

Some games may save non-sensitive gameplay data, such as scores, rankings, session results, or anonymous player IDs. This data is used to provide gameplay features and improve the games.

Our games are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and remove it if necessary.
